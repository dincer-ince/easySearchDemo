Easy Search
Demo showcase of Easy Search API capabilities

EasySearch is a complete document management solution aimed for both personal and commercial use. The solution as well as being a stand alone application, it can also be integrated into any tech stack that can reach internet.

EasySearch easily processes big documents, and becomes a powerful search engine that searches with whatever algorithm you prefer.

By creating clusters of document it also allows you to make automatic categorization of documents
